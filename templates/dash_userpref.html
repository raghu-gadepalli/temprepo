Trade Management Guide

User Preferences now manage user/account flags only: broker login, enabled instruments, watchlist, intraday mode and autotrade.

Stops and targets are lifecycle-managed. Each trade stores its active state in trade_management, including current protective stop, current adaptive target, ATR basis, risk reduction and expansion state.

This keeps trade behavior consistent across users and makes tuning happen in lifecycle config instead of per-user fixed stop/target fields.
